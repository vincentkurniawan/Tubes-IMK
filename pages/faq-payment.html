Frequently Asked Payment Questions

What if I want a refund?
Easy! If you need a refund you will need to contact our customer service first. To contact our customer service, you can send an email to johnbooks.cs@gmail.com regarding the problem at hand.
Do you sell gift cards?
Sorry, at this time we do not provide gift card services.
Do you provide cash on delivery payment methods?
Sorry, at this time we do not provide cash on delivery payment services.
What payment methods do you accept?
For now we accept payments with VISA, Mastercard, Paypal, and DANA. In the future, other payment methods will be available.
Do you have an offline store so I can buy books directly?
Sorry, because of the COVID-19 pandemic, we do not provide on-site book purchases. For now you can only buy our books online.
